Help & FAQ

How to use?

  • Add Duty: Click the big "Add Today" button on Home or tap any date on the calendar. (Free)
  • Edit/Delete: Tap a date with an entry to open the menu, then change details or click Delete. (Free)
  • Check-in/out: Use the Check-in/Check-out buttons on home screen. (Free)
  • Advance: Go to Advance tab to record money taken. It will be deducted in reports. (Premium Feature)
  • PDF & Salary Calculation: Go to Reports, select dates, and click Download PDF. (Premium Feature)
  • Utility Rates: Salary, OT rates configuration is Premium only. (Premium Feature)
  • Free Premium: Join our Telegram and WhatsApp channels to get 7 days free premium!
  • PIN Security: Set a 4-digit PIN during signup. Use phone + PIN to login anytime.
